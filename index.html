Projects

Solo Project: Water you up too?
Have you ever been playing at a park with your four legged friend and ran out of water? Maybe you needed to use a restroom but didnt know where to look. The problem that I am solving with this mobile website is to help users find public water access when in a public park or out and about. This app will locate public water fountians, restrooms, and pet friendly restaurants closest to your location. Giving this normally annoying problem a quick and easy fix.

Group Project: SVC
My team and I worked with the Sexual Violence Center in Minneapolis to rebuild thier current internal website. This website is used for government reporting for funding. The current website was no longer reporting correct information, needed to keep the non-profit open. We built a new website from scratch using React.js for the Frontend, Node, and Express for the Backend, and Postgres Sql for the database. We also imported all older data into the new database and build a custom query page in case a new reporting is needed in the future.
